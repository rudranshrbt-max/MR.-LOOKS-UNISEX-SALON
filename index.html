About Mr. Looks Unisex Salon

Mr. Looks Unisex Salon is a premium unisex salon in Patrakar Colony, Indore, offering haircut, hair color, keratin and smoothening for hair; facial, cleanup and skin-glow treatments for skin; manicure, pedicure and nail art; and party and bridal makeup. Located opposite Baskin Robbins Icecream, the salon is easy to find and easy to reach for walk-ins and appointments alike. Whether you're after a sharp everyday cut, a full hair transformation, or a complete bridal look, Mr. Looks is built to deliver a consistent, premium experience every visit. Call ahead or message on WhatsApp to book your slot.

Terms & Conditions

Please review before booking your appointment.

Appointments can be booked via WhatsApp, Instagram, phone call, or this website. We recommend booking at least a few hours in advance for regular services, and 2–3 days in advance for bridal, party makeup, or treatment packages to secure your preferred artist and time slot.

We request a minimum of 24 hours’ notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours’ notice, or no-shows, may result in forfeiture of any advance payment made for the appointment.

For bridal services, skin/hair treatments, and packages, an advance payment of 30–50% of the estimated service cost is required at the time of booking to confirm your slot. The remaining balance is payable on the day of the service.

No refunds will be issued for services that have already been completed. Refunds on advance payments for cancelled bookings are handled on a case-by-case basis, subject to the cancellation notice period being honoured.

Information shared during booking (name, contact number, service preferences) is used solely to manage your appointment and communicate with you about your visit. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

All tools and equipment are sanitized between every client. Disposable items (such as nail files, applicators, and razors) are single-use only. Our staff follow standard hygiene and grooming protocols to ensure a safe, clean environment for every visit.
